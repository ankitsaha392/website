JMS

Research Journal Management System

In this project, I created a Journal Management System that facilitates the submission, review, and editing of manuscripts for academic journals. Authors can submit their manuscripts through the system, which are then accessible to editors. Editors can view the submitted manuscripts, assign reviewers based on expertise, and manage the review process. Reviewers can access assigned manuscripts, provide comments and feedback, and contribute to the decision-making process. This system streamlines the journal management process, making it more efficient and collaborative for all parties involved.

In this project, we developed a Garbage Collector application that allows users to schedule garbage collection based on their preferences.
